How do we work?

1: Intake

We like to hear what the wishes and expectations are and like to think along. We prefer to do this on location. We provide a cost estimate.

2: Measuring

We measure everything, and prepare everything, ask for details such as materials and finish.

3: Construction, finishing.

We have cut everything to size and painted locally if desired, and come to assemble the cabinet. Everything will likely be to your liking and you can enjoy a new eye-catcher in your home.

Who are De Ruimtemannen?

De Ruimtemannen are Anielson and Reinoud, two craftsmen with a shared passion for solid carpentry.

Anielson has 8 years of experience in construction and spent 3 years learning the trade from an experienced carpenter. This combination of broad building knowledge and deep carpentry expertise makes him an all-round craftsman who knows how it should be done.

Reinoud sold his company and decided it was time to work more with his hands. He traveled to Switzerland for an intensive course with a highly skilled carpenter, and is now Anielson's steady right-hand man and has completed many assignments.

Together we take on jobs with precision, craftsmanship, and personal attention, so we are sure that you will be satisfied.
